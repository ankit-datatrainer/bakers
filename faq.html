Help centre

Frequently asked questions

Can't find an answer? Contact us or ping us on WhatsApp.

What areas do you deliver to?
We deliver all over Delhi. Enter your area at checkout and we'll confirm the delivery slot — most orders arrive the same day, freshly baked and safely packed.
Is there a minimum order or delivery fee?
No minimum order! Delivery is ₹49, and free on orders above ₹499.
How much notice do custom cakes need?
Simple custom cakes need 24 hours. Themed or multi-tier cakes need 48–72 hours. WhatsApp us a reference photo and we'll confirm feasibility and price within the hour.
Do you have eggless options?
Yes! Many of our treats are fully eggless — look for the "Eggless" badge on the menu. Most cakes can be made eggless on request at no extra charge, just mention it in your order.
What payment methods do you accept?
UPI, credit/debit cards and cash on delivery.
How do I track my order?
After checkout you'll receive an order ID (like GB3X9K2A). Enter it on the Track Order page to see live status.
What if my order arrives damaged?
Send us a photo on WhatsApp within 2 hours of delivery and we'll refund or replace it, no questions asked.
Do you take bulk or corporate orders?
Absolutely — hampers, meeting boxes and event catering from 10 to 1,000 people. Email gracebakersofficial@gmail.com with your date and headcount for a quote within 24 hours.
How should I store my bakes?
Cookies and dry cakes: keep in an airtight box at room temperature for up to a week. Celebration cakes and brownies: refrigerate and enjoy within 48 hours; rest at room temperature for 15–20 minutes before serving.
Do you list allergens?
All products are made in a kitchen that handles gluten, dairy and nuts. Item-specific allergen info is available on request — mention allergies in your order notes and we'll call you before baking.
