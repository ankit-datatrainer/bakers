Our story

Baked with love, since 2015

What started as one home oven and a sourdough starter named "Bubbles" is now Rohini's favourite neighbourhood bakery.

How it began

From a home kitchen to 50,000 happy customers

In 2015, founder Meera Kapoor began baking sourdough for neighbours from her Sector 7 apartment. Word spread faster than butter on warm toast. Today our 14-person team bakes 36+ items fresh every single morning — but every recipe still starts the same way: real ingredients, slow fermentation, and zero shortcuts.

We're proudly a 100% vegetarian, FSSAI-certified kitchen, and we still name every new sourdough starter. (The current one is "Dough-nald.")

Our values
